Blake Lively Wore a Shacket With Nothing But a Bra Underneath

Publish date: 2024-02-07

Leave it to Blake Lively to make even the most casual outfit look chic and sexy. The actress stepped out for a stroll in New York City looking comfy in her version of sweats, which included a pair of joggers and a shacket.

Blake wore a brown-and-black checkered button-down, which she left open to reveal her Alo Wellness Bra. She paired her top with black, slim-cut joggers and white sneakers.

She kept her accessories to a minimum with a singular dainty ring and a crossbody BandolieriPhone case. The star appeared to be taking a call as she spoke into her phone and sported AirPods during her walk.

The sighting comes after she and her husband, Ryan Reynolds, stepped out for a date night earlier this month at the premiere of his new sci-fi flick, The Adam Project. For that event, Blake wore a multi-colored whimsical Versace gown. The film, which also stars Jennifer Garner, Mark Ruffalo, and Zoe Saldaña, will be released on Netflix on March 11.

Prior to the premiere, the power couple announced on social media that they would be matching donations made to the United Nations Refugee Agency up to $1 million in support of Ukrainian refugees. Blake posted about the generous contribution on her Instagram account with a photo of small child in winter gear and shared more information in her caption.

"@usaforunhcr is on the ground helping the 50,000+ Ukrainians who had to flee their homes in less than 48 hours," Blake wrote. "@usaforunhcr is providing life saving aid, and also working with neighboring countries to ensure protection for these families."
