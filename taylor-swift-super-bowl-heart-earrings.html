We Found the Cutest Dupe of Taylor Swift's Super Bowl Heart-Shaped Earrings

Publish date: 2024-03-06

Whether you're celebrating Galentine’s or Valentine’s Day, or both, you’ll need some jewelry for the occasion. It just so happens that Taylor Swift gave us the perfect accessory inspiration on Super Bowl Sunday — an adorable heart-shaped earring. While the singer celebrates with her beau— aka Travis Kelce and the entire Kansas City Chiefs team (congrats to them!)— we tracked down where Swift bought that shiny heart earring.

The Heart Twist Earring in white diamond and carnelian 14K yellow gold by The Last Line sells for $495 each. That means that if you want two, it’ll be just under a thousand dollars. The pricing is due to the fact that the jewelry is hand carved and sculpted into a heart-shaped stone and set with diamonds. Plus, just look at it. It’s stunning.

Kate Spade New York Sweetheart Huggie Hoop Earrings

Kate Spade New York Sweetheart Huggie Hoop Earrings

While Taylor Swift’s budget is not feasible for many of us, we found the perfect dupe from kate spade. The Sweetheart Cubic Zirconia Drop Hugie Hoop Earrings not only come as a set (phew!), but they're also perfect for a self-care splurge or a sweet Valentine’s gift. These hugging hoop earrings run for $68 in the color red multi. We can totally see Taylor wearing these too, by the way.

Swift paired a multitude of accessories with her outfit, including three beautiful gold rings (all set with a variety of red stones), an “87” necklace as a nod to Kelce, and a gorgeous ruby necklace with a matching bracelet. As for the outfit, the superstar sported a black corset top, crystal slit jeans, a Chiefs bomber jacket (is that style making a comeback?), and classic black ankle boots also adorned with the number “87.” It’s very safe to say that Taylor was in the Super Bowl spirit, as she completed the look with a red lip.

In case you missed it, Swift posted Kelce for the first time on her social media in a joking video about taking her parents to the club (honoring the big win, of course!). If you see this Taylor, we would also like the inside scoop on your Valentine’s Day plans. Please and thank you!
