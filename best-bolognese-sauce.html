Best Bolognese Sauce &amp; Vegan Bolognese to Buy

Publish date: 2024-09-08

Jarred bolognese sauce was a real mystery to me. I know soups and pastas filled with meat are canned and jarred all the time, but something about a meaty, premade bolognese sauce sitting on a grocery store shelf weirded me out. I can’t explain it! Anyway, I’m happy to report that my concerns were unfounded. The best bolognese sauce at the grocery store is actually delicious.

Homemade bolognese sauce is great, but we found the best bolognese sauce to buy when you don’t have time to cook. Just because you’re busy doesn’t mean you don’t deserve a delicious Italian meal!

Most Surprising

Fody Vegan BologneseAmazon

We haven’t loved Fody’s other products, so we didn’t have high hopes for their plant based bolognese sauce. Their whole thing is that they make products for people who have stomach issues. As an IBS survivor, I want to like their products. I appreciate the work they’re doing. But, as to be expected, when you take essential ingredients like onions and garlic out of food, it doesn’t really taste as good. Somehow, though, they’ve made a delicious, sensitive stomach-friendly bolognese. It’s free of meat but full of flavor, and it’s one of the best bolognese sauce choices you can make. It’s also wonderfully oily like Rao’s iconic marinara sauce. I would definitely buy this jarred bolognese sauce again. It’s truly one of the best store bought bolognese sauces out there, especially if you don’t eat meat.

Best Plant Based

Whole Foods 365 Plant-Based BologneseAmazon

I’ll be real with you, this plant based bolognese sauce from Whole Foods doesn’t really have the texture of bolognese. There’s nothing particularly meaty about the way it looks or the mouthfeel. Despite that, it’s still among the best bolognese sauce we tried in our taste test. Even if it isn’t meaty, per se, there’s a super strong, savory, umami flavor in this vegan bolognese. It’s almost mushroom-y. If you are looking for a plant based bolognese that is truly reminiscent of the real meaty deal, buy this. It’s the best vegan bolognese, and one of the best jarred bolognese sauce options in general.

Best of the Best Runner Up

Just Like Home Chicago-Style BologneseAmazon

This was our favorite premade bolognese sauce. The flavor is so unique and deep and rich. There’s tons of meat. It’s super acidic. In our opinion, it’s a perfect 10/10! Why isn’t it in the number one spot on this list of the best bolognese sauce, you might ask? Because it’s a bit of a departure from most premade bolognese sauce. It’s made with pork sausage rather than ground beef. And one of its most predominant flavors is fresh bell pepper. It’s probably not exactly what people are looking for when they’re buying store bought bolognese sauce. Even though it might not be what you’re expecting, I promise it’ll be what you’ll want after you try it.

Best of the Best

Rao’s Homestyle BologneseInstacart

Rao’s Homemade is the king when it comes to sauces. They make several marinara sauces that are truly incredible. Their arrabbiata rules. And, perhaps predictably, their premade bolognese sauce is outstanding. It’s the best bolognese sauce to buy if you want traditional bolognese. I know we really hyped up Just Like Home, but we also adore Rao’s. This sauce is oily and rich and super meaty. Once you try this premade bolognese sauce, you will probably not make homemade bolognese again.
