➡ Nicholas Hamilton Biography

Nicholas Hamilton is an Australian actor, who became famous for his outstanding, magnificent performances throughout the career. He has been so passionate and loyal to his profession since long. 8 Things You Didn’t Know About Nicholas Hamilton

His Birth & Family Background

Nicholas Hamilton was born on 3rd May 2000, Lismore, Australia to Vicki Atkins & Craig Hamilton. He raised and grew up with his siblings as he has a brother named, Joshua Hamilton and two sisters named, Rebecca & Rachel Hamilton. 

His Career Beginning

Nicholas William Hamilton played his first role as Elvis in a school play. It was the time when he was in grade fifth. He really enjoyed the play and the character he played. Later, he joined a local acting agency to hone his acting skills.

He Is Best Known For

He has worked in various movies and television series but Nicholas Hamilton is best known for playing the role of Rellian in “Captian Fantastic”, and also Henry Bowers in “It and It Chapter Two”.

Nicholas Hamilton credits his Uncle For

Nicholas William Hamilton always credits his uncle as starting his career. In 2011, his uncle died from cancer who helped him to start his career.

His Television Debut

In 2013, Nicholas Hamilton made his television debut with a series named “Mako: Island of Secrets” in which he played the role of Ben and became famous.

His Film Debut

Nicholas Hamilton has worked in many films and has won millions of hearts with stellar performances but has made his acting debut with the movie named “Time”, released in 2013. In the movie, he played the role of James.

Awards & Honors

The young Australian star has honored with various awards and titles including “Best Actor” award for the movie “Time” at “Tropfest Short Film Award” in 2013. Also, he was nominated for various roles for the best movies.

He Loves Pets

Nicholas Hamilton is presently living in Australia and has no plans to move to Los Angeles. He loves pets so he spends a great time with them.
