Tiktok Star Grandma Holla aka Helen Davis Passes Away

Publish date: 2024-06-12

Famous TikTok star Grandma Holla is reported dead on 15-01-2023.

The Unlikely 2022 breakout sensation was Grandma Holla. The world learned she was suffering a fatal illness during the height of her run. Grandma Holla was the subject of death rumors over the summer.

The information shocked the social media community. Grandma Holla became a celebrity last year thanks to her devoted followers and the stars. Sadly, this time the news of Grandma Holla’s dying was not embellished.

At the end of the previous week, Grandma Holla’s condition deteriorated. There were reports that she had died when the news broke. Grandma Holla was still alive, the family said at the time, but they requested respect and prayers.

Numerous tributes poured in since the sudden demise of Grandma Holla, Social media users could not hide their shock at her death news.
