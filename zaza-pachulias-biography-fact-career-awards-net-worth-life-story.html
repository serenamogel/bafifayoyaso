Zaza Pachulia is a Georgian basketball player with a net worth of $13 million. He plays for the Golden State Warriors of the National Basketball Association. He was born as Zaur Pachulia but had his name legally changed to Zaza. He was able to win the NBA championship with the Warriors in 2017 and 2018.

Zaza still holds his Turkish citizenship and plays for Georgia internationally and works as the captain of his national team in various tournaments.

Pachulia got drafted in the second round by the Orlando Magic in the 2003 NBA draft. He got selected in the expansion draft of the Charlotte Bobcats in 2004 and got traded to the Milwaukee Bucks. Here, he averaged with 5.1 rebounds and 6.2 points for every game off the bench. He began playing for the Golden State Warriors in the year 2016.
