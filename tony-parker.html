Tony Parker Biography

Tony Parker was born in Bruges, Belgium, but spent most of his youth in France. The family of Parker’s father has a long history of involvement in gaming. His uncle had taken home an Olympic rowing medal. Parker and his brothers have always shared a tight bond. Together, they used to watch their father’s sporting events.

In his younger years, Parker dreamed of playing football. But everything changed when he witnessed Chicago’s Michael Jordan blossoming into a basketball legend. This caused him to reconsider. Parker discovered via his skill-development sessions that he excelled at the point guard position.

He became aware of his agility and speed and soon concluded that this position was perfect for him in every game. Tony spent the following two seasons playing in French amateur levels before signing with Paris Basket Racing in 1999.
