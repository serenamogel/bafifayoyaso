Why does my Maytag washer say Loc?

Publish date: 2024-10-09

Answer

LoC or LC – Front Load Washer. This error code does not mean “Door Locked”. The door will open when the control is locked, but the washer will not start, due to control being locked.

Accordingly, what does loc mean on Maytag washer?
