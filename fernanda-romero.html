Biography

Fernanda Romero was born in Mexico City, Mexico on October 4, 1983. Mexican actress, singer and model who got her start in Mexican television before getting her big break in Hollywood in 2008, when she starred in the horror film The Eye. She has since gone on to appear in more American films, including The Burning Plain, Red Canvas and Drag Me to Hell. She co-starred with Jessica Alba in The Eye. As a teenager, she was a member of the band Frizzby, who performed for Pope John Paul II in her hometown of Mexico City. By age 18, she was modeling for brands like Apple and Pepsi.
