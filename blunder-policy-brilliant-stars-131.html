Blunder Policy (Brilliant Stars 131)

Publish date: 2024-09-14

Blunder Policy
からぶりほけん Miss Insurance

Blunder Policy (Japanese: からぶりほけん Miss Insurance) is a Pokémon Tool card. It is part of the Brilliant Stars expansion.

Card text

Attach a Pokémon Tool to 1 of your Pokémon that doesn't already have a Pokémon Tool attached.
If the Pokémon this card is attached to uses an attack, if you flip any coins for the damage or effect of that attack, and if any of them are tails, draw 3 cards at the end of your turn.
You may play any number of Item cards during your turn.

Release information

This card was included in the Brilliant Stars expansion, first released as one of the Japanese S-P Promotional cards. In Japan, it was one of seven cards available in Pokémon Card Gym Promo Card Pack 7, which was distributed at Pokémon Card Gym venues able to do so from September 2021. This print features the Pokémon Card Gym logo.

Trivia

Based on the release schedules of previous Pokémon Card Gym Promo Card Packs, it is likely this card would have been available alongside the dual Skyscraping Perfection and Blue Sky Stream expansion on July 9, 2021. However, due to the delay of Promo Card Packs 5 and 6 amid COVID-19 restrictions, Promo Card Pack 7 did not become available until September. As a result, this card was officially released first in Taiwan and Hong Kong as a Traditional Chinese-language S-P Promotional card in early August 2021.

Origin

The Blunder Policy is a held item in the Pokémon games that raises the holder's Speed by two stages if one of their moves misses because of an accuracy or evasion check.
