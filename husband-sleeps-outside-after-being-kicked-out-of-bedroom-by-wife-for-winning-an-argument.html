Husband Sleeps Outside After Being Kicked Out Of Bedroom By Wife For Winning An Argument

Publish date: 2024-05-26

According to an account by a Twitter user known as Young Kemt, a married man, won a fight with his wife and was then forced out of the bedroom by his wife.

After he and his wife had a disagreement, the man, was kicked out of the bedroom and was forced to sleep outside the room for that night.

He shared photos of himself sleeping on the verandah of their house as he lays on a small bed. His experience has triggered hilarious reactions from social media users.

In other news, a broken-hearted Nigerian guy has narrated how his girlfriend took advantage of him to help her get ready for her traditional wedding.

The man explained that his fiancée had informed him that her sister was getting married, so he helped by driving her to the store to get the wedding-related necessities.

Later, the man drove his girlfriend’s sister and himself from Onitsha to Nsukka before returning the same day.

When he later called to ask how the planning for the sister’s wedding was progressing, she said that everything was fine.

But when he logged into Facebook the following morning, after waking up and seeing photographs of his girlfriend being married and people congratulating her, he was thrown into complete disbelief.
