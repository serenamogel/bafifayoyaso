Publish date: 2024-07-03

Who is Wayne Newton?

Wayton Newton is a well-recognized American singer and actor. Newton is widely famed as one of the best-recognized entertainers in Las Vegas.

Moreover, some of his popular songs consist of 1972’s “Daddy, Don’t You Walk so Fast”, “Years”, and “Red Roses for a Blue Lady”.

How old is Wayne Newton?

Wayne Newton was born on 3 April 1942. At present, he is an 80-year-old star. Likewise, his birthplace is Norfolk, Virginia, the USA.

Wayne is the son of his father, Patrick Newton whereas his mother is Evelyn Marie Smith. Moreover, he has an older brother named Jerry.

Talking about his nationality, Newton holds an American identity whereas his ethnicity is English, Irish, Scottish, Welsh, and German.

Regarding education, the star completed his higher study at North High School.

Wayne Newton- Professional Life, Careers

Wayne Newton began his career in a support role for a different role which he earned from different icons. He then earned a gold disc by the R.I.A.A. in July 1972 for his recording of “Daddy, Don’t You Walk So Fast” that was sold over one million copies. Further, the song reached #1 in Australia.

Likewise, on 23 May 1989, Newton’s live stage show was aired as a pay-per-view event named Wayne Newton Live in Concert. The actor then hit #1 on the Cashbox Pop and Country charts with an Elvis Presley-inspired song, “The Letter”.

The entertainment star succeeded Bob Hope as chairman of the USO Celebrity Circle in 2001. He then began his reality television show on E! in January 2005 named The Entertainer. Moreover, he was the grand marshal of the 80th Annual Shenandoah Apple Blossom Festival in Winchester, Virginia.

Freshly, in June 2020, Wayne appeared on television commercials in North America being a spokesperson for Caesars Entertainment to support the reopening of Caesars Entertainment resorts at the time of the COVID-19 pandemic.
