Enuka Okuma Net Worth

Publish date: 2024-07-27

Enuka Okuma:

$3.5 million

Enuka Okuma is a Canadian actress whose career started in the early 1990s. She first starred as Kelly in the teen drama Hillside. She later had recurring roles in Madison, Da Vinci’s Inquest, and The City. In 1999, she made her film debut in Double Jeopardy. In the 2000s, she had roles in the movies Josie and the Pussycats, Suddenly Naked, House of the Dead, and Stolen. She also became known for her role as Lucy Dotson in the television series Sue Thomas: F.B.Eye. From 2010 to 2015, she became further known for starring as Traci Nash in Rookie Blue. Following this, she had performances in the films Battle of the Sexes and The Sleepover. She also starred in the web series Impulse, as well as appeared in some episodes of the TV drama A Million Little Things.
