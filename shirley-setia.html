Shirley Setia is a multi-skilled lady who is not just an actress, yet a great singer as well. She is basically from New Zealand and has worked in various domains, like; acting, singing, etc. She is mainly known for her acting debut “Nikamma”. She did graduation from the University of Auckland and also did an internship in marketing at Auckland Council.

Career

In 2014, she started her career in the field of music and dropped a single “Thode Se Hum” and sung with co-singer Abhas Joshi. Since then, she frequently released various albums and singles for which she got immense love and recognition. She participated in a contest that was conducted by T-Series. Also, she worked as a disc jockey as a part-time while studying. She has recorded a video for YouTube in which she donned a PJs for which she earned a nickname “Pyjama Popstar”. Moreover, she has worked in a movie named “Maska” which was released in 2020. She also collaborated with other foreign stars and worked with them. However, she has established herself as the best multi-skilled artist who flaunts her skills.

Achievements

She has earned lots of titles and awards, but she considers her high fan base following on social media platforms as the greatest achievement of life.
