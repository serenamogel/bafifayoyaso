Moomoo Milk (known until HeartGold & SoulSilver as Moo-Moo Milk) (Japanese: モーモーミルク MooMoo Milk) is an Item card. It was first released in the Neo Genesis expansion as a Trainer card.

Card text

Neo Genesis print
Choose 1 of your Pokémon. Flip 2 coins. Remove 2 damage counters times the number of heads from that Pokémon. If the Pokémon has fewer damage counters than that, remove all of them.
HeartGold & SoulSilver print
Choose 1 of your Pokémon. Flip 2 coins. For each heads, remove 3 damage counters from that Pokémon.
Lost Thunder print
Choose 1 of your Pokémon, and then flip 2 coins. For each heads, heal 30 damage from that Pokémon.

e-Reader data

The Expedition Base Set print includes a Dot Code strip for use with the e-Reader, and has the card ID A-29-#. The short strip contains a brief card summary. There is no long strip for this card.

Release information

This card was included in the Japanese Gold, Silver, to a New World... expansion with artwork by Tomokazu Komiya. It was later included in the English Neo Genesis expansion with different artwork by the same artist. The illustration of the Japanese print depicts a Sentret suckling on a cow-like udder, which prompted a change for releases outside Japan as it was arguably seen as inappropriate for the TCG's primary audience. Subsequent Japanese prints, starting with the Pokémon VS expansion, used the artwork from the Neo Genesis print. It was reprinted again in the Expedition Base Set, first released in the Japanese Pokémon-e Starter Deck.

Moomoo Milk was reclassified in Japan as an Item card during the HeartGold & SoulSilver Era, though the English Trainer cards did not adopt this naming convention until the Black & White Series. The print included as part of the HeartGold & SoulSilver expansion features new artwork by Noriko Hotta, which was first released in the Japanese SoulSilver Collection. It was reprinted in the Japanese Steelix Constructed Standard Deck. It was then reprinted in the Raichu Half Deck of the HS Trainer Kit.

Due to the fact that this card's HeartGold & SoulSilver print's name and effect slightly differ from the original, it was ruled that "Moo-Moo Milk" and "Moomoo Milk" were not the same card in the English-language printings. A similar situation exists surrounding Energy Charge and Power Charge.

Moomoo Milk received a new print in the Sun & Moon Series as part of the Lost Thunder expansion with new artwork by Ayaka Yoshida, first released in the Japanese Super-Burst Impact expansion. In Japan, it was reprinted as one of the SM-P Promotional cards and was 1 of 9 possible cards included in special packs sent to Event Organizers in April 2019 for distribution in official events hosted by them. This print features the Event Organizer logo in the bottom right corner of the illustration.

Trivia

The original print spells the item's name with a hyphen. In the Pokémon games, it is spelled as "Moomoo Milk" (previously "MooMoo Milk") without a hyphen.

Origin

Moomoo Milk is a nutritious drink in the Pokémon games that can restore 100 HP to the Pokémon it is fed to.
